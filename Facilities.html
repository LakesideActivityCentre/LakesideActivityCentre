      MORE FACILITIES DISCRIPTIONS

Gym

Our fully equipped gym offers everything you need to stay fit during your stay. With state-of-the-art machines, free weights, and a variety of cardio equipment, you can enjoy a complete workout experience. Open daily with extended hours for your convenience.

Outdoor Patio

Relax and unwind on our beautiful outdoor patio, a perfect space for enjoying fresh air and stunning views. Comfortable seating, garden views, and a tranquil setting make it an ideal spot to connect with nature or simply take a break from your day.

Spa

Indulge in ultimate relaxation at our spa, where tranquility and rejuvenation await you. From soothing massages to luxurious facials, our spa offers a wide range of treatments designed to refresh both body and mind. Our expert therapists ensure you leave feeling renewed and restored.

Business Center

Stay productive with ease in our fully equipped business center. Featuring high-speed internet, printing services, and a comfortable workspace, it’s the perfect place for business travelers to work on the go. Stay connected and efficient during your stay.
